What Grassion does

Complete visibility
into your config layer

Stop flying blind. Know every variable, its risk level, its usage, and what breaks if you change it.

🗺️
Variable Map
Auto-generates a complete graph of every environment variable across all services — where it's used, what it controls, how it flows.
💀
Dead Detection
Instantly identifies variables that exist in your .env but are never read by any code. Clean up safely with zero guesswork.
🔀
Drift Comparison
Compares production vs staging vs local environments. Shows exactly where configs diverge and why that's dangerous.
⚠️
Risk Scoring
Every variable gets a risk score based on criticality, exposure, and change frequency. Know what to protect before it's too late.
📜
Git History Intelligence
Traces config changes through git history. Understand who changed what, when, and correlate it with incidents automatically.
🔔
Real-time Alerts
Get Slack/Teams notifications when a critical variable changes or drifts. Catch problems before they reach production.
