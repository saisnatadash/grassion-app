What Grassion does

Complete visibility
into your config layer

Stop flying blind. Know every variable, its risk level, and what breaks if you change it.

🔊
Variable Map
Auto-generates a complete graph of every env variable — where it’s used, what it controls, how it flows across services.
💀
Dead Detection
Instantly identifies variables that exist in your .env but are never read. Clean up safely with zero guesswork.
🔀
Drift Comparison
Compares prod vs staging vs local. Shows exactly where configs diverge and why that’s dangerous.
⚠
Risk Scoring
Every variable gets a risk score based on criticality, exposure, and change frequency.
📜
Git History Intelligence
Traces config changes through git history. Understand who changed what and correlate with incidents.
🔔
Real-time Alerts
Slack/Teams notifications when a critical variable drifts. Catch problems before they hit production.
How it works

From chaos to clarity
in 4 steps

No agents, no production access. Works entirely in your browser.

01
Upload your .env
Drop your .env file into the scanner. Works with any stack. Nothing is sent to our servers.
02

Grassion analyses your config in under 30 seconds and builds the full variable risk map.
03
Review insights
See risk scores, dead vars, drift alerts, and service mappings in one unified view.
04
Ship confidently
Download your Pro report. Know what’s safe to change. Share with your team or security officer.
